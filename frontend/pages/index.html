User Guide

Welcome to the TikTok Music Recommender! Follow these steps to find the perfect music for your videos:

  1. Add Tags: Use the input box to add tags related to your video content. Click "Add Tag" after typing each tag.
  2. Search for Songs: Once you've added your tags, click the "Search for songs" button to find music that matches your tags.
  3. Play Music: Click "Play" on any music card to listen to the track.
  4. Identify Music: Click "Identify Music" to see its details on Spotify or YouTube.
